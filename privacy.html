1. Introduction

Welcome to EasyPG ("we," "us," "our," or "Company"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application available on the Google Play Store.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you use EasyPG, we collect information that you voluntarily provide, including:

Account Registration

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Date of birth (for staff and tenant records)

Property Management Information

  • Property name and address
  • Room configurations and bed counts
  • Property-specific details and metadata

Tenant/Student Records

  • Student name, email, and phone number
  • Date of birth
  • Family information (parent/guardian names and phone numbers)
  • Emergency contact information
  • Room and bed assignment details
  • Tenure status (Active, Paused, Removed)

Staff Management Data

  • Staff member name, position, and contact information
  • Attendance records (Present, Absent, Half-Day)
  • Salary and wage information
  • Salary advances and payroll details
  • Employment status and archive records

Financial Records

  • Rent payment transactions
  • Payment dates and amounts
  • Rent collection and pending amounts
  • Staff payroll settlements

Maintenance Information

  • Maintenance complaints and descriptions
  • Complaint categories (Electrical, Plumbing, Housekeeping, Security, Other)
  • Complaint status and resolution timestamps
  • Complaint history and archives

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use our application, we automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifier
  • Usage Data: Features accessed, actions performed, timestamps, duration of use
  • Log Data: Application crashes, errors, and technical performance metrics
  • Location Data: Property addresses provided by users (not GPS tracking)
  • Network Information: Connection status and network type for diagnostic purposes

2.3 Information from Third Parties

We do not deliberately collect information from third-party sources. However, your information may be processed through our infrastructure partners listed below.

4. Data Security and Storage

4.1 Data Storage

  • Cloud Infrastructure: Your data is stored on Supabase-managed PostgreSQL servers with redundant backups
  • Encryption: Data is encrypted both in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest using industry-standard encryption protocols
  • Local Caching: For offline functionality, sensitive data is cached locally on your device in a secure manner

4.2 Access Control

  • Row-Level Security (RLS): Implemented at the database level to ensure users can only access data associated with their own properties
  • Authentication: Multi-layer authentication verifies user identity before granting access
  • Session Management: User sessions expire automatically after 30 days for security

4.3 Security Measures

  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Compliance with industry security standards
  • Employee access controls and confidentiality agreements
  • Automatic backup systems to prevent data loss

4.4 Limitations

While we implement comprehensive security measures, no system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information, though we maintain strict standards to protect your data.

6. Data Retention

6.1 Retention Periods

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained as long as your account remains active
  • Deleted Accounts: Upon account deletion, personal data is retained for compliance purposes for a minimum of 7 years (as per applicable tax and legal requirements)
  • Archived Records: Historical data (removed tenants, staff, completed complaints) is retained for audit and compliance purposes
  • Deleted Records: Backups may retain deleted data for up to 30 days for disaster recovery

6.2 Data Purging

After retention periods expire, data is securely deleted or anonymized and cannot be recovered.
